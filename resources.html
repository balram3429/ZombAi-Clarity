Attention Is All You Need
The original transformer paper by Vaswani et al. Introduced the architecture that powers all modern LLMs. Required reading.
Google Brain58k+ citations
 📄 Paper · 2020
RAG: Retrieval-Augmented Generation
The original RAG paper from Meta AI. Introduced the paradigm of combining retrieval with seq2seq generation.
Meta AIFoundational
 📄 Paper · 2022
ReAct: Reasoning + Acting in LLMs
Introduced the ReAct prompting strategy for interleaving reasoning traces and actions in LLM agents.
Google / PrincetonAgents
 📄 Paper · 2021

The paper behind Stable Diffusion. Introduces latent diffusion models — run diffusion in compressed latent space.

OpenAI's technical report on GPT-4 capabilities, safety evaluations, and alignment approaches.
